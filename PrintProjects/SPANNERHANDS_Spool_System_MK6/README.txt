# Summary

A completely enclosed spool holder, with a desiccant sachet clip.

Spool Compatibility
MasterSpool Version 4
Spool Core ID 52.5mm - 55mm
Spool OD max 205mm
Spool Width max 82mm
Can be used as a wall mounted spool holder in 2 orientations or free standing on a work-surface.

Please let me know of any other mounting options your require and I will get that added to the design.

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Prusa
Printer: Prusa Mk2
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: 200 Microns
Infill: 30%

Notes: 
Quality Printing Tips:

Pay particular attention to the thin walls of the main body, you want to make sure you match your extrusion width (0.43mm works best with s 0.4mm nozzle) so you get a wall that's just 4 perimeters with no infill. The reason you want to do this is; if your slicer tries to infill inside the thin walls not only will it add loads of print time you end up with a poor quality print.
