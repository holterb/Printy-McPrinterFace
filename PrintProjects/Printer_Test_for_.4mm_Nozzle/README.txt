# Summary

Printer and filament test model specifically designed for .4mm nozzles. 

(Note: Two slightly different sized nuts are included in model to account for varying filament tolerances. These nuts may be screwed onto the model's threaded post after printing.) 

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Prusa
Printer: i3 MK3
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: Up to 200 microns.
Infill: 100%
