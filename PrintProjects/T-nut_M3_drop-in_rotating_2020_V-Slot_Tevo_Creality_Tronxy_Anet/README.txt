# Summary

T-nut for 20x20 or 20x40 V-Slot aluminum extrusion (6 mm gap) compliant with original metal T-nut but adopted for **M3** nut.

There's also a version that fits to #4-40 x 3/16'' nut:
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3366563

Fits for most of the printers based on V-Slot, e.g. Creality (CR-10, Ender 3, Ender 5), Tevo (Tarantula, Tornado), Tronxy (X1, X3, X5S) and many more

Put it in the profile slot and turn the bolt - it will turn and find its locking position.

Check out my other designs:
https://www.thingiverse.com/DaVinci2000/designs

A decent set of M3 hex bolts and nuts can be found on Banggood:
https://www.banggood.com/custlink/KG3vEvGOIp

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: TEVO
Printer: Tarantula
Rafts: Doesn't Matter
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.2
Infill: 100%
Filament_brand: Any
Filament_color: Any
Filament_material: PETG/ABS

Notes: 
Print very slow around 10 mm/s with a little (if single nut) or no cooling (batch) ideally with PETG or ABS, as those are not so brittle as PLA. 

For best results and strength set in Cura:
Top Layers: 1000
Bottom Layers: 0
Top/Bottom Pattern: Concentric
