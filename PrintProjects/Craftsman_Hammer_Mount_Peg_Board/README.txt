# Summary

I wasn't happy with any of the hammer mounts available (none of them were asymmetrical so the hammer would hang crooked). I made this one in Fusion 360 with a 5mm offset and it holds my Craftsman hammer perfectly straight. 

Printed in PETG at .2mm layer height, with supports.
