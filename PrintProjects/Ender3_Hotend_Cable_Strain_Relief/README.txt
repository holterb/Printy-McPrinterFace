# Summary

This cable relief greatly stabilizes extruder tube and wires to the hotend, prolonging service life of especially the wires.

Fits the Ender3 hotend by removing two M3 screws and attaching the part by using two M3 x 10 mm screws, without removing the hotend cover. 

Add a couple of cable ties and you're done.
