# Summary

Test cube for new filaments.
It's designed to print fast, use only a small amount of filament and give you a good first impression of what you can expect your finished prints to look like.

Includes different features like small holes & pins, overhangs, bridges and small letters.

Printtime between 30 minutes and 1 hour.
Uses about 5 grams / 2 meters of 1.75mm filament.

Measurements:
20mm side length
Horizontal hole: 10mm diameter
Vertical holes/pins: 6mm/2mm diameter, 10mm height/depth
Rectangular hole (Bridge test): 5mm width, 4mm height

# Print Settings

Printer: Prusa i3 MK2
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.2mm
Infill: 20%
